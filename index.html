Our intuitive chest-based shop system empowers you to establish your own dynamic marketplace. Buy and sell items directly with other players, fostering a truly player-driven server economy.

How to Create Your Shop:

  1. Place a **chest** at your desired shop location.
  2. **Left-click** the chest to open the shop creation interface.
  3. Follow the on-screen prompts to set your selling item(s) and their prices.

How to Buy/Sell from a Shop:

  • **PC:** Simply Left-click the chest.
  • **Mobile:** Press the sign bord above chest.

You'll then be prompted to enter the desired quantity for your transaction.
